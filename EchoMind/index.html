EchoMind

Record a thought. Get a clean note.

EchoMind is an iOS voice-first notes app in active development. It captures spoken thoughts and turns them into transcripts, summaries, and clearer next actions.

One-tap capture On-device + AI workflows Transcription, summary, translation
This is not the final version. UI and feature depth are actively improving over time.

Current prototype flow

  • Home screen with one-tap recording and quick entry points for ideas, study, and tasks.
  • Library with recordings, timestamps, and duration previews.
  • Recording details with playback, transcription, and summary blocks.
  • Translate actions for transcript and summary from the same screen.
  • Settings for model selection, language, sounds, haptics, storage, and export defaults.

AI output today

The current output in screenshots already shows structured interpretation beyond plain text conversion.

  • Detects likely reference types (for example plans/schedule, humor/phrase).
  • Highlights date and time cues found inside free speech.
  • Supports quick re-transcribe and re-summarize iterations.
  • Adds translation hooks to keep notes accessible across languages.

What is coming next

The near-term direction is to make EchoMind more dependable for daily planning and multilingual note workflows.

  • Cleaner recording organization and faster search.
  • More robust extraction of tasks, dates, and reminders.
  • Stronger sharing/export combinations for audio + text summaries.
  • Ongoing UI refinements driven by real usage feedback.
