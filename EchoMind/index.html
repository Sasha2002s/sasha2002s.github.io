EchoMind

Record a thought. Get a clean note.

EchoMind is an iOS voice-first notes app in active development. The current prototype already captures thoughts fast and converts speech into structured output you can review and reuse.

Current prototype flow

  • Home screen with one-tap recording and quick entry points for ideas, study, and tasks.
  • Library screen with saved recordings, timestamps, and duration previews.
  • Recording details with playback, full transcription, summary, and translate actions.
  • Re-transcribe and re-summarize controls for better output after model or language changes.
  • Settings for haptics, sounds, transcription model/language, storage, and export behavior.

Early transcript intelligence is already visible in the screenshots: EchoMind can detect likely references and extract time-related cues from spoken text to make follow-up actions easier.

Product status: this is not the final version.

UI will keep improving over time, and more functions will be added as the workflow matures.
